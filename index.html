Why Spelman?


Why is Spelman the #1 HBCU for the 10th straight year?

Spelman continues to be the #1 HBCU because of its passion and willingness to educate and provide for
African American women, so that we be successfully equipped for a society so pitted against us.

Spelman embodies womanhood, sisterhood, and a safe haven for women of all sizes, shapes, ethnicity,
to feel like they are equal and their opinions and views matter.

Why is Computer Science a good major?

Computer science is a good major because it's a major that dives into the most innovative, and evolving
part of our society. Emersing oneself into technology, and how it runs and operates can cultivate our future,
and how our society will adapt and create ways to make every day tasks more sufficient.
